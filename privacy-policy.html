Privacy Policy

Swiggy Guilt Score (Guilt Calculator)

Last updated: June 15, 2026

👀 What the extension does

Swiggy Guilt Score reads food item names displayed on swiggy.com and zomato.com pages to estimate the approximate calorie content of items in your cart or on the menu, and shows you a "guilt score" based on that estimate.

🔒 What we collect NOTHING

This extension does not collect any data. Specifically, it does not:

  • Access or read your personal information (name, email, address, payment details, etc.)
  • Use cookies
  • Use tracking or analytics tools
  • Create user profiles or accounts

⚙️ How it works

All processing — reading food item names and estimating calories — happens entirely within your browser, on your device. No information about the pages you visit, the food items you view, or your browsing activity is ever sent to a server, stored remotely, or shared with anyone.

🤝 Third parties

This extension does not share data with, or rely on, any third-party services, APIs, or advertising networks. There are no third parties involved in any way.
